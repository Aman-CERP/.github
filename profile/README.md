## The Problem

Enterprise software is broken.

Legacy ERPs require 6-18 month implementations, armies of consultants, and users who simply *endure* rather than enjoy the software. Small and mid-sized businesses are forced to choose between overpriced complexity, fragmented point solutions, or outdated systems that feel like they were designed in 1995.

There has to be a better way.

---

## Our Belief

> **"Of course, this is how I expected it to work."**

Business software should disappear into the background, empowering people to focus on what truly matters. We're building AmanERP on three principles:

| Principle | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Intuitive over powerful** | If users are surprised by how something works, we've failed |
| **Zero-config start** | The system works correctly the moment you create an account |
| **The system is the truth** | One data model. Not 45 apps stitched together |

---

## What We're Building

AmanERP is a **compound startup** - a suite of integrated products on a shared core data layer from day one.

```
Organization
  Finance        Invoicing, expenses, accounting
  People         Directory, payroll, time tracking
  Operations     Inventory, procurement, workflows
  Intelligence   Unified search, AI insights, reporting
```

### Technical Foundation

- **Multi-tenant by design** - True isolation with shared infrastructure efficiency
- **Fine-grained authorization** - Relationship-based access control
- **Event-driven architecture** - Real-time everything
- **AI-first** - Not an add-on, but woven into every interaction
- **Cloud-native** - Built for scale from line one

## Open Source Engines

We're building AmanERP on a foundation of **open-source engines** - reusable, production-grade components that solve hard infrastructure problems.

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Infrastructure engines** | Rate limiting, workflow orchestration, job queues |
| **Compliance engines** | Audit logging, consent management, policy enforcement |
| **Developer tools** | MCP servers, SDKs, CLI utilities |

**The model:** We build engines. We mature them in production. We open source them.

## The Name

**Aman** means *peace, safety, protection* in Hindi/Sanskrit.

**CERP** = Cloud Enterprise Resource Planning

We're building software that gives business owners peace of mind - knowing their operations, finances, and teams are managed in one secure, reliable system.
